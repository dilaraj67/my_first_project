## GIG STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

### 1. GIG TITLE (Max 80 characters)
**Formula:** "I will [action verb] [specific deliverable] for [target audience]"

**Good Examples:**
- "I will do accurate data entry and Excel work for your business"
- "I will write SEO blog articles for your website or blog"
- "I will create professional social media graphics for Instagram"
- "I will be your virtual assistant for admin tasks and research"

**Bad Examples:**
- "I will do anything" (too vague)
- "Best data entry expert worldwide!!!" (spammy)
- "Data entry" (not descriptive)

### 6. REQUIREMENTS FROM BUYER

**Be specific about what you need:**

‚úì Clear, specific questions
‚úì Make required vs optional clear
‚úì Avoid asking for too much upfront

**Good Examples:**
- "Please describe the task in detail" (Required)
- "Attach any files or examples" (Optional)
- "What is your preferred deadline?" (Required)
- "Any specific formatting requirements?" (Optional)

**Bad Examples:**
- "Tell me everything" (too vague)
- "Attach 10 different file types" (too demanding)

---

### 7. TAGS (5 tags max)

Use exact phrases buyers search for.

**How to find good tags:**
1. Type your service in Fiverr search
2. See what autocompletes
3. Check competitor gigs
4. Use those exact phrases

**Example: Data Entry Gig**
- data entry
- excel expert
- copy paste
- virtual assistant
- typing jobs

### 8. GIG EXTRAS (Upsells)

Add extras buyers can purchase:

**Examples:**
- Extra fast delivery (+$10) - 24h instead of 3 days

- Extra pages/items (+$5 per unit)
- Commercial use license (+$15)
