## üìÖ DAILY WORKFLOW (30-60 minutes)

### **OPTION A: Full Hour (Recommended)**

**Step 1: Open LinkedIn (5 min)**
- Have **quick_reference_guide.md** open in another tab
- Have **company_targets.csv** open to know who to look for

**Step 2: Find People (10 min)**
- Search for 5 people using searches from quick reference guide
- Examples:
  - "Harvard Kennedy School" + [target company]
  - "International Monetary Fund" + [target company]
  - [Target company] + "Middle East analyst"

**Step 3: Write Messages (20 min)**
- Open **message_templates.md**
- Pick the right template for each person
- Customize with specific details (their background, company news, etc.)
- Reference **your_profile_summary.md** to remember what to emphasize
- Send all 5 messages

**Step 4: Update Tracker (5 min)**
- Add all 5 people to **outreach_tracker.csv**
- Set calendar reminders for follow-ups (7-10 days)

**Step 5: Apply to Jobs (15 min)**
- Check career pages from **company_targets.csv**
- Apply to 2-3 jobs
- Add to **job_applications_tracker.csv**

**Step 6: Follow-Ups (5 min)**
- Check if anyone responded
- Send follow-up messages to people from last week (if needed)
- Schedule calls if anyone said yes

---

**10 min:** Find 3 people on LinkedIn
**10 min:** Send 3 messages using templates
**5 min:** Update outreach tracker
**5 min:** Check for responses and follow-ups
