# Freelance Platforms - Direct Sign-up Links

## START HERE (Easiest to get started)

### Global Platforms - Sign up immediately:

1. **Upwork** - https://www.upwork.com/
   - Click "Sign Up" → Choose "Work" (as freelancer)
   - Most active platform with daily jobs

2. **Fiverr** - https://www.fiverr.com/
   - Click "Join" → "Become a Seller"
   - Create gigs (services you offer)

3. **Freelancer.com** - https://www.freelancer.com/
   - Click "Sign Up" → Choose freelancer account
   - Bid on projects immediately

4. **PeoplePerHour** - https://www.peopleperhour.com/
   - Free to join, less competition than Upwork

5. **Guru** - https://www.guru.com/
   - Create seller profile
   - Apply for jobs in your category

6. **Contra** - https://contra.com/
   - **0% commission** - Keep 100% of earnings
   - Create portfolio and get hired

## QUICK START ACTION PLAN

**Today (Next 2 hours):**
1. Sign up for Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer.com
2. Complete your profile 100% on each (photo, bio, skills, portfolio)
3. On Upwork: Apply to 5-10 jobs with personalized proposals
4. On Fiverr: Create 3 gigs for services you can deliver

**Skills in High Demand (Easy to start):**
- Data entry
- Virtual assistant
- Content writing
- Social media management
- Translation (if bilingual)
- Graphic design (Canva templates)
- Research and web scraping
- Customer service

**Tips to Get First Job:**
- Start with lower rates to build reviews
- Write personalized proposals (not copy-paste)
- Respond within 1 hour of job posting
- Offer a small free sample or discount on first order
- Apply to 10-20 jobs per day minimum

**Payment Setup:**
- Create Payoneer account: https://www.payoneer.com/
- Links to Upwork, Fiverr, Freelancer for international payments
- Can withdraw to your local bank
