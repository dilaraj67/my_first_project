## Key Features of This Template:

1. **Strong Opening (First Two Sentences)**: Immediately establishes why you're right for the role with specific years of experience, regional expertise, and relevant skills
2. **Quantifiable Achievements**: Includes specific metrics ($X million, X reports, X% accuracy) to demonstrate impact
3. **Demonstrates Knowledge**: References actual Eurasia Group work and current macro themes
4. **Shows Public Speaking**: Mentions speaking engagements, which is part of the role
5. **Highlights Requirements**: Languages, in-country experience, elite education, analytical skills
6. **One Page Maximum**: Structured to fit on one page when properly formatted
7. **Professional Tone**: Balances confidence with humility
